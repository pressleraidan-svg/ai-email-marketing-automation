# Project Summary

## Overview

This project demonstrates the planning and execution of a complete lifecycle email marketing strategy for a fictional outdoor retailer, Summit Supply Co.

The objective was to create an automated email marketing program that would improve customer engagement, increase conversions, and strengthen long-term customer relationships.

Rather than focusing on individual emails, the project approaches email marketing as an ongoing customer journey supported by segmentation, automation, analytics, and continuous optimization.

---

## Goals

- Increase first-time purchases
- Improve customer retention
- Recover abandoned carts
- Build customer loyalty
- Improve email engagement
- Use data to optimize campaign performance

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Email Marketing
- Marketing Automation
- CRM Strategy
- Customer Segmentation
- Lifecycle Marketing
- Marketing Analytics
- A/B Testing
- AI-Assisted Marketing
