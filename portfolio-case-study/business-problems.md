# Business Problem

Summit Supply Co. has experienced consistent website traffic but lower-than-expected conversion rates.

Several opportunities for improvement were identified:

- Visitors frequently abandoned their shopping carts.
- New subscribers rarely completed a first purchase.
- Existing customers purchased infrequently.
- Marketing emails lacked personalization.
- Customer engagement declined over time.

The company wanted a scalable email marketing strategy that could improve customer experience while increasing revenue.
