# Customer Research

The marketing strategy was developed using three primary customer groups:

## First-Time Visitors

Interested in learning more about the brand and products before purchasing.

---

## Returning Customers

Already familiar with the company and more likely to respond to personalized recommendations.

---

## Loyal Customers

High-value customers who respond well to exclusive offers and early product access.

Understanding these audiences allows campaigns to deliver more relevant messaging throughout the customer journey.
