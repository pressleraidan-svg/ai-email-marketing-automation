# 📧 AI Email Marketing Automation

**By Aidan Pressler**

A portfolio project demonstrating how a modern email marketing program can use customer segmentation, lifecycle marketing, automation, analytics, and AI-assisted planning to improve customer engagement and drive business growth.

This project was created to showcase practical marketing skills that are commonly used in email marketing, CRM, growth marketing, and marketing automation roles.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Successful email marketing goes far beyond sending newsletters. It requires understanding customer behavior, delivering the right message at the right time, and continuously improving campaigns through testing and analytics.

This project walks through the planning and documentation of a complete lifecycle email marketing strategy for a fictional outdoor retailer, including customer personas, segmentation, automated email journeys, campaign KPIs, A/B testing, and AI-assisted workflow planning.
