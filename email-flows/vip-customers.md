# VIP Customer Journey

## Objective

Reward loyal customers while increasing long-term retention.

---

## Trigger

Customer exceeds a lifetime spending threshold or completes multiple purchases.

---

## Email 1

### Thank You

Recognize customer loyalty.

Offer exclusive early access to new products.

---

## Email 2

### VIP Rewards

Offer exclusive discounts and member-only promotions.

---

## Email 3

### Product Preview

Invite VIP customers to shop before public product launches.

---

## Success Metrics

- Customer Lifetime Value
- Repeat Purchases
- VIP Revenue
- Customer Retention
