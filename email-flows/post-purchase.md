# Post-Purchase Campaign

## Objective

Strengthen customer relationships after a purchase while encouraging repeat business.

---

## Trigger

Customer completes an order.

---

## Email 1

**Send:** Immediately

### Purpose

Thank the customer and confirm their order.

### CTA

Track Your Order

---

## Email 2

**Send:** 7 Days Later

### Purpose

Provide product tips and best practices.

### CTA

Learn More

---

## Email 3

**Send:** 14 Days Later

### Purpose

Request a product review.

### CTA

Leave a Review

---

## Email 4

**Send:** 30 Days Later

### Purpose

Recommend complementary products.

### CTA

Shop Recommended Products

---

## Success Metrics

- Repeat Purchase Rate
- Product Reviews
- Customer Satisfaction
- Average Order Value
