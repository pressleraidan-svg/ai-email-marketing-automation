# Win-Back Campaign

## Objective

Reconnect with inactive customers and encourage them to return.

---

## Trigger

No purchases within the past 90 days.

---

## Email 1

**Subject**

We Miss You

Purpose:

Reconnect with the customer and showcase new arrivals.

---

## Email 2

**Subject**

Here's 15% Off Your Next Order

Purpose:

Provide an incentive to return.

---

## Email 3

**Subject**

Last Chance to Save

Purpose:

Create urgency before the offer expires.

---

## Success Metrics

- Reactivation Rate
- Revenue Recovered
- Email Open Rate
- Conversion Rate
