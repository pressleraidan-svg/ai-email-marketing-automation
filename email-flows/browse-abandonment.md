# Browse Abandonment Campaign

## Objective

Reconnect with visitors who viewed products but left without adding anything to their cart.

---

## Trigger

A visitor views one or more product pages but leaves the website without making a purchase.

---

## Email 1

**Send:** 24 Hours Later

### Purpose

Remind the customer about the products they viewed and encourage them to return.

### Content

- Recently viewed products
- Customer reviews
- Product benefits

### CTA

Continue Shopping

---

## Email 2

**Send:** 3 Days Later

### Purpose

Recommend similar products based on browsing behavior.

### Content

- Personalized recommendations
- Best sellers
- New arrivals

### CTA

Discover More

---

## Success Metrics

- Return Visits
- Click-Through Rate
- Product Views
- Conversion Rate
