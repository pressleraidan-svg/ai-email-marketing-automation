# Welcome Series

## Objective

Introduce new subscribers to the brand, build trust, and encourage a first purchase.

---

## Trigger

A visitor subscribes to the email newsletter.

---

## Email 1 — Welcome

**Send:** Immediately

### Purpose

- Thank the subscriber
- Introduce the brand
- Set expectations for future emails

### CTA

Shop Best Sellers

---

## Email 2 — Brand Story

**Send:** 2 Days Later

### Purpose

- Share company values
- Highlight product quality
- Build credibility

### CTA

Explore Our Collection

---

## Email 3 — Social Proof

**Send:** 5 Days Later

### Purpose

- Showcase customer reviews
- Highlight popular products
- Reduce purchase hesitation

### CTA

See Customer Favorites

---

## Email 4 — First Purchase Incentive

**Send:** 7 Days Later

### Purpose

Offer a limited-time first purchase discount.

### CTA

Claim Your Discount

---

## Success Metrics

- Open Rate
- Click Rate
- First Purchase Conversion
- Revenue Generated
