# Abandoned Cart Email

**Campaign:** Cart Recovery

**Audience:** Customers who added products to their cart but did not complete checkout.

---

## Subject Line Options

- You Left Something Behind
- Your Gear Is Waiting

---

## Preview Text

Complete your order before your items sell out.

---

## Email

Hi {{First Name}},

It looks like you left a few items in your cart.

Don't worry—we saved them for you.

Complete your order today and get back to planning your next adventure.

Need more time?

Your cart will remain available for a limited period.

Click below to continue shopping.

**Return to Cart**

Questions?

Our customer support team is always happy to help.

---

## Goal

Recover abandoned carts and increase completed purchases.
