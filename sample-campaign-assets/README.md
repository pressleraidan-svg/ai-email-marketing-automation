# Sample Campaign Assets

This folder contains examples of the documents and assets that an email marketing specialist might create while planning, executing, and optimizing lifecycle email campaigns.

These materials are intended to demonstrate campaign planning, customer communication, performance tracking, and marketing documentation.

---

## Folder Contents

- Welcome Email
- Abandoned Cart Email
- Monthly Newsletter
- VIP Customer Email
- Campaign Calendar
- Campaign Performance Report
- Monthly Dashboard

These examples are based on the fictional outdoor retailer used throughout this project.
