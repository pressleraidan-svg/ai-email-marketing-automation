# AI Email Marketing Automation Case Study

## Overview

This project demonstrates how I would build and optimize an AI-assisted email marketing program for a fictional outdoor retailer called **Summit Supply Co.**

The purpose of this project was to apply real-world email marketing principles while using AI to improve productivity, content development, and campaign planning.

Although the company is fictional, every workflow and strategy is based on techniques used by modern email marketing teams.

---

# Business Challenge

Summit Supply Co. receives strong website traffic but experiences three common challenges:

- High abandoned cart rates
- Low repeat purchases
- Limited customer personalization

The objective of this project is to improve customer engagement throughout the customer lifecycle using automated email campaigns.

---

# Goals

- Increase first-time purchases
- Recover abandoned carts
- Improve customer retention
- Increase customer lifetime value
- Personalize customer experiences
- Use data to continuously optimize performance

---

# My Approach

I designed several automated email journeys based on customer behavior, including:

- Welcome Series
- Abandoned Cart
- Browse Abandonment
- Post-Purchase
- Win-Back Campaign
- VIP Customer Journey

Each campaign includes clear objectives, timing, messaging strategy, and calls-to-action.
