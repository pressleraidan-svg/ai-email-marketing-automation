# Customer Segmentation Strategy

Customer segmentation allows email campaigns to deliver relevant messaging based on customer behavior rather than sending the same email to everyone.

---

## New Subscribers

Trigger:
- Signs up for newsletter

Receives:
- Welcome Series
- Brand introduction
- Best-selling products
- First purchase incentive

---

## Browsing Customers

Trigger:
- Views multiple products without purchasing

Receives:
- Product recommendations
- Educational content
- Customer reviews

---

## Cart Abandonment

Trigger:
- Adds items to cart but leaves website

Receives:
- Reminder email
- Social proof
- Limited-time discount
- Final reminder

---

## First-Time Customers

Trigger:
- Makes first purchase

Receives:
- Thank you email
- Product tips
- Review request
- Cross-sell recommendations

---

## Repeat Customers

Trigger:
- Makes multiple purchases

Receives:
- Loyalty rewards
- VIP offers
- Early access promotions

---

## Inactive Customers

Trigger:
- No purchases in 90 days

Receives:
- Win-back campaign
- Personalized recommendations
- Exclusive offer
