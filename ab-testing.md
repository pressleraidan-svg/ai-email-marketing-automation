# A/B Testing Strategy

Testing is one of the most effective ways to improve email marketing performance. Rather than making assumptions, marketers can compare different versions of an email and use data to determine what performs best.

---

## Test 1: Subject Line

Version A:
> You left something behind.

Version B:
> Your camping gear is waiting.

**Goal:**
Increase open rates.

---

## Test 2: Call-to-Action

Version A:
> Shop Now

Version B:
> Complete Your Order

**Goal:**
Increase click-through rate.

---

## Test 3: Send Time

Morning:
9:00 AM

Evening:
6:00 PM

**Goal:**
Determine when subscribers are most likely to engage.

---

## Metrics to Track

- Open Rate
- Click-Through Rate
- Conversion Rate
- Revenue per Email
- Unsubscribe Rate

---

## Conclusion

The winning version would become the default campaign while new tests would continue to improve performance over time.
