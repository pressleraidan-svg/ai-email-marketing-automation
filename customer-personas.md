# Customer Personas

Understanding different customer types allows marketing campaigns to be personalized based on interests, purchase behavior, and engagement.

---

# Persona 1: Adventure Alex

### Overview

- Age: 27
- Occupation: Software Engineer
- Income: $78,000
- Shops primarily on mobile
- First-time outdoor enthusiast

### Goals

- Find reliable outdoor gear
- Learn camping basics
- Stay within budget

### Pain Points

- Doesn't know which gear to buy
- Price sensitive
- Easily overwhelmed by too many choices

### Marketing Strategy

- Educational welcome emails
- Beginner buying guides
- Product recommendations
- Limited-time discounts

---

# Persona 2: Family Frank

### Overview

- Age: 42
- Married with children
- Household income: $120,000
- Purchases outdoor equipment several times each year

### Goals

- Durable equipment
- Family-friendly products
- Quick purchasing experience

### Pain Points

- Limited time
- Needs dependable products
- Wants long-lasting value

### Marketing Strategy

- Bundle promotions
- Seasonal campaigns
- Loyalty rewards
- Product comparison guides

---

# Persona 3: Weekend Wendy

### Overview

- Age: 33
- Young professional
- Frequently browses products
- Rarely completes purchases

### Goals

- Discover new products
- Compare prices
- Wait for promotions

### Pain Points

- Cart abandonment
- Hesitates before purchasing
- Easily distracted

### Marketing Strategy

- Browse abandonment emails
- Cart recovery campaigns
- Personalized recommendations
- Flash sales
