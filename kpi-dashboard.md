# KPI Dashboard

## Overview

Measuring campaign performance is essential for understanding what is working, identifying opportunities for improvement, and making data-driven marketing decisions.

The metrics below represent sample performance goals for Summit Supply Co.'s email marketing program.

---

## Campaign Performance

| KPI | Target | Why It Matters |
|------|:------:|----------------|
| Open Rate | 42% | Measures subject line effectiveness |
| Click-Through Rate | 8% | Shows how engaging the email content is |
| Conversion Rate | 4% | Tracks purchases resulting from email campaigns |
| Revenue per Email | $1.85 | Measures financial impact |
| Cart Recovery Rate | 18% | Tracks abandoned cart success |
| Repeat Purchase Rate | 22% | Measures customer retention |
| Unsubscribe Rate | Less than 0.5% | Indicates subscriber satisfaction |

---

## Monthly Goals

- Grow email subscribers by 10%
- Increase repeat purchases by 15%
- Improve click-through rate through A/B testing
- Reduce cart abandonment
- Increase customer lifetime value

---

## Reporting Schedule

Weekly:
- Open Rate
- Click Rate
- Revenue
- New Subscribers

Monthly:
- Customer Retention
- Campaign ROI
- Customer Lifetime Value
- Overall Email Performance

---

## Key Takeaway

Marketing decisions should be guided by measurable results. Tracking these KPIs allows campaigns to be continuously optimized through testing and customer insights.
