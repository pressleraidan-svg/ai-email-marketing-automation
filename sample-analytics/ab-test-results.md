# A/B Test Results

## Test Objective

Determine whether a personalized subject line improves open rates.

---

## Version A

Subject:

Welcome Back!

Open Rate:

38.4%

---

## Version B

Subject:

Aidan, Your Camping Gear Is Waiting

Open Rate:

45.9%

---

## Winner

Version B

---

## Key Takeaways

Including the customer's first name increased engagement and produced a statistically significant improvement in open rates.

Future campaigns should continue testing personalized messaging.
