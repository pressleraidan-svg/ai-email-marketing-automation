# AI Prompt Library

Throughout this project, AI was used as a productivity tool to speed up brainstorming, generate ideas, and improve marketing workflows. Every output was reviewed, edited, and refined before being included in the project.

---

## Prompt 1: Welcome Email

**Objective:**
Create a welcoming first impression for new subscribers.

**Prompt:**

> Write a friendly welcome email for an outdoor retailer introducing the brand, highlighting popular products, and encouraging a first purchase without sounding overly promotional.

---

## Prompt 2: Subject Line Ideas

**Objective:**
Increase email open rates.

**Prompt:**

> Generate five subject line variations for an abandoned cart email. Use urgency without sounding pushy.

---

## Prompt 3: Customer Segmentation

**Objective:**
Create audience groups based on shopping behavior.

**Prompt:**

> Suggest customer segments for an outdoor e-commerce brand based on purchase history, browsing behavior, and engagement.

---

## Prompt 4: A/B Testing

**Objective:**
Develop two versions of an email campaign to compare performance.

**Prompt:**

> Write two versions of a promotional email with different calls-to-action to test which approach generates more clicks.

---

## Takeaway

AI works best as a collaborative tool. It helps speed up research, idea generation, and drafting, while human judgment ensures the final content matches the brand's voice and marketing goals.
